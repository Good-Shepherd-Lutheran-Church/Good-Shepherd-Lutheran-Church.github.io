2023-2024 Tuition Rates and Rules

Tuition is payable in 9 installments, and payments are due 10 days after billing date every month beginning August and ending April. A late fee of $25.00 will be added if not paid on time. A fee of $25.00 will be added for all returned checks. If tuition is not paid within 5 days after the late fee is assessed, the child may be withheld from class until the bill is paid in full.
